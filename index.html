About the Development

New Harbour is set to transform Old Harbour into a thriving, modern community while preserving the ease and comfort of its surroundings. The development will introduce a vibrant town centre with 50+ commercial spaces, including a supermarket, pharmacy, restaurants, a bank, medical offices, food stores, and an office park.

With direct access to the new highway, the project offers quick connections to Kingston, Spanish Town, and nearby communities. It is the first major project of its kind in Old Harbour in over 40 years.
